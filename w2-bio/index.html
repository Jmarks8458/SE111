Bio

I grew up in Massachusetts in the town of bourne. Bourne is the town situated right on the Cape Cod Canal.
I've also got family in Florida as well as Canada. In the past my father used to own a ranch in Colorado.
We had alpacas, horses, goats, a donkey, and even an awesome pig. She was very friendly and would
roll over for belly rubs like a dog. Despite the ranch being in the Colorado desert, it was actually quite nice.
With the cleanest, naturally filtered water in the ground pumped by wells, and the most ripe strawberries I've
ever had it was surprisingly pleasant for a land of sage and sand.
